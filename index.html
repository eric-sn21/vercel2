网站宗旨

Purpose of This Website

笔者创建这个网站的起因是念及当年筹备交换时在互联网上四处搜罗攻略的辛苦，决意将笔者这一年从申请开始的完整的交换流程、材料、见闻，都细细整理在这。一来为笔者留个实在的记录，二来也教那些初涉欧洲申请的学弟学妹或是有需要者能有个经实践考验可行的经验，少走些摸索时的弯路。虽不敢说有多大裨益，但如是能抛砖引玉，在实处帮到大家便觉得这份力并不曾落到空处。

I started this website because I remember how hard it was to find good information when preparing for my exchange program. I decided to organize everything here: the full application process, required documents, and what I learned from studying and living there. This does two things – it keeps my own records clear, and more importantly, gives future students a real-world guide to avoid the same struggles I had. It might not be perfect, but if it helps even a little or gets others sharing their experiences too, then putting this together was worth it.

Visa Application Process

荷兰学签申请需要通过学校ISD向荷兰政府递交学前申请。在成功激活RUG邮箱后会收到ISD发来的有关申请MVV和Residence Permit的邮件，里面会包含完整的申请流程。

Dutch student visa application needs to be submitted through the school's ISD to the Dutch government. After activating your RUG email, you will receive an email from ISD regarding MVV and Residence Permit application with complete instructions.

Housing Application Guide

荷兰住房资源非常紧缺，但是RUG会通过和SSH为交换生保证一部分的住房，需要在SSH网站上填写相关信息注册Short Stay账户后即可预定房间（floor plan可参照35是8楼）。

Housing resources are very limited in the Netherlands, but RUG guarantees some housing through SSH for exchange students. You can book a room after registering a Short Stay account on the SSH website (floor plan reference: 35 is on the 8th floor).

请一定注意抢房子开始的时间，抢到预定的房子后按照网站上的指示缴费即可。

Please pay attention to the room booking start time. After securing a room, follow the website instructions for payment.
